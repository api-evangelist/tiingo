# Tiingo

> Tiingo provides high-quality financial market data APIs across US equities, crypto, FX, fundamentals, and news: end-of-day prices back to 1962, IEX and BOATS intraday data, consolidated equity realtime snapshots, crypto and crypto-yield data, forex, SEC-sourced fundamentals, fund fees, corporate actions, and ticker-tagged news, over REST and WebSocket at api.tiingo.com. Authentication is a single account API token (?token= or Authorization: Token header). All plans are usage-limited by hourly/daily requests and monthly bandwidth.

## APIs

- [End-of-Day API](https://www.tiingo.com/documentation/end-of-day): Daily OHLCV history for 100,000+ US equities and ETFs back to 1962
- [IEX Intraday API](https://www.tiingo.com/documentation/iex): IEX-sourced intraday quotes and trades, REST and WebSocket
- [Crypto API](https://www.tiingo.com/documentation/crypto): Cross-exchange crypto top-of-book, trades, and history
- [Forex API](https://www.tiingo.com/documentation/forex): Real-time and historical FX rates
- [Fundamentals API](https://www.tiingo.com/documentation/fundamentals): SEC-sourced statements and daily metrics
- [News API](https://www.tiingo.com/documentation/news): Ticker- and topic-tagged news from 1,000+ publishers
- [Corporate Actions](https://www.tiingo.com/documentation/corporate-actions/dividends): Dividend distributions and splits
- [Equity Realtime (beta)](https://www.tiingo.com/documentation/equity-realtime-stock-data): Consolidated multi-venue realtime snapshots
- [BOATS Overnight (beta)](https://www.tiingo.com/documentation/boats): Blue Ocean ATS overnight session equity data
